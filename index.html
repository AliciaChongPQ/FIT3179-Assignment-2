Water-Borne Disease Incidence in Malaysia (2018-2021):

Trends and Public Health Impact

Typhoid: Cases of typhoid fever have fluctuated, with some areas experiencing notable outbreaks. This is often linked to contaminated drinking water and poor food hygiene.

Hepatitis A: Hepatitis A, commonly spread through contaminated water or food, has seen periodic spikes in states with inadequate sanitation services.
